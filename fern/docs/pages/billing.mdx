ListBee charges a platform fee on each sale. The fee rate depends on your plan. Stripe processing fees are separate and billed directly by Stripe.

---

## Plans

| Plan | Platform fee | Monthly cost | Listings |
|------|-------------|--------------|----------|
| Free | 10% | $0 | 50 |
| Growth | 5% | $29 | Unlimited |
| Scale | 3% | $79 | Unlimited |

### Zero-decimal currencies (price in major units)

For JPY and KRW, set `price` to the whole number — not cents. `price: 500` means ¥500, not ¥5.

| Code | Currency | Minimum price |
|------|----------|---------------|
| JPY | Japanese Yen | 100 |
| KRW | South Korean Won | 500 |

## Fee calculation

The platform fee is deducted from each sale via Stripe's `application_fee_amount`.

```
platform_fee = price × fee_rate
```

Rounding uses banker's rounding (round half to even).

**Example — USD, Free plan (10%)**

```
price:          1000  (= $10.00)
fee_rate:       0.10
platform_fee:   100   (= $1.00)
seller receives: 900  (= $9.00, before Stripe processing fees)
```

**Example — JPY, Growth plan (5%)**

```
price:          2000  (= ¥2000)
fee_rate:       0.05
platform_fee:   100   (= ¥100)
seller receives: 1900 (= ¥1900, before Stripe processing fees)
```

---

## Stripe processing fees

Stripe charges its own processing fees (typically 2.9% + 30¢ for US cards) directly to your connected Stripe account. These are separate from ListBee's platform fee and not configurable through ListBee.
